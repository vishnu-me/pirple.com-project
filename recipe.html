Samosha

Make the perfect flaky and crispy Samosa at home! All tips and tricks to make the perfect samosa each time! These are filled with a spicy potato filling and are also vegan.
A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, or lentils. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region. The Indian style, often accompanied by a chutney, is probably the most widely known of a broad family of recipes from Africa to China, which have origins in medieval times or earlier. Samosas are a popular entree, appetizer, or snack in the local cuisines of the Indian subcontinent, Western Asia, Southeast Asia, the Mediterranean, and Africa. Due to emigration and cultural diffusion from these areas, samosas today are often prepared in other regions.
